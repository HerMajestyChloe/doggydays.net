Five must have amazon products for your dog!

Have you recently adopted a dog? Or maybe you are just thinking about taking the plunge into pet ownership. Perhaps, you’ve had a dog for a short or long time. If any of the above describes you, then these amazon products will be something you want to get your hands on as soon as possible! Today we will be taking a dive into some pet product must-haves that you can find on amazon!

Number 5:

My number 5 pick is probably the least exciting to you, but your furry friend will greatly appreciate this item when it comes to training! These Pet training clickers from HoAoOo (What a name) are perfect for getting started on your pet training journey. With a loud audible click, your pet won’t have any trouble hearing it at all. Not only do you get 2 of these clickers for only $5, but they also come with wristbands, so you don’t drop or miss place them during training.

These clickers are shaped like an egg and fit comfortably inside your hands. They also feel quite durable and I would feel comfortable knowing it would still work if I dropped it a few times by accident. I read through the majority of the reviews on what appears to be a solid product with 4.5 stars and was shocked to see so many 1-star reviews. Looking at the 1-star reviews specifically showed that most people don’t understand how to properly use the clicker and simply blamed user error on the product.

This next product is for daily use! It will surely make those morning walks much better!

Number 4:

If you’ve read my article on leash training, you will know that I despise collars with a passion. Not only are they dangerous and lead to many easily avoidable pet fatalities, but they are also the least secure way of walking your pet. Take comfort in knowing that by using a harness you are ensuring your pet can’t get loose and will be staying by your side injury free!

This harness comes in 14 colors and has a 4.5 out of 5-star review. I was shocked to see that 76% of these reviews were 5 stars whereas only 3% were 1-star reviews. Reading through the few 1-star reviews I noticed a lot of people saying that smaller dogs have an easier time slipping out of these harnesses. Make sure to size your dog appropriately to ensure a snug fit. One person mentioned their dog would pull the harness backward and slip right out. If this is able to happen, you simply have the wrong size.

The kid in me loves this one with a passion.

Number 3:

Have you ever wanted to sit in your lawn chair on a summer evening with the family, but want to keep your dog happy and occupied? Are your children having trouble throwing the ball far enough to play fetch with the family dog? Then this is for you! With a 4.3 star rating out of 5 on amazon this will be a fun toy for the family and the dog!

Reading through some of the reviews I noticed that one person had a very clever idea of using tennis balls with squeakers inside! This way the dog will be interested in the toy longer. The majority of 1-star reviews on this product were complaints that the 12-inch blaster had balls that were too small for bigger dogs. I’m not sure why they would think that buying the smallest blaster they make would be ideal for their dog when it goes all the way up to a 20-inch that can launch actual tennis balls.

Do you frequently find yourself driving around with your pet? Me too! Keep them safe and secure with this doggy seat belt.

Number 2:

When I first got my Porsche Boxster I was worried I’d never get to take my dog on a ride with the top down. I’ve heard many horror stories of dogs jumping out at traffic stops or even when going down the road. With this product, I no longer have to worry! It comes with 2 seat belt adapters that will attach to your dogs harness (Do not use these with a collar). You can attach one to the seat belt and the other will hook like a car seat anchor to the base of the seat. This will keep your fur baby safe and in place! With only 3 one star reviews, the most common issue seems to be that the seat belt attachment may become bent over time.

The number one spot for this list is a must-have for all dog breeds.

Number 1:

Does your dog enjoy laying on a bed? Do you have to purchase a new one each week? Well, this product aims to fix that. Made of mesh and quite comfortable, this bed is breathable and will be sure to keep your doggo cool in the summer. It’s perfect for indoor and outdoor use, you can take it outside when needed and leave it inside for the dog to lounge on.

The reviews on this product seemed solid with 4.5 out of 5 stars. One-star reviews were few, and all seemed to be about the same. If you do purchase this product, make sure to inspect the mesh carefully as it may be ripped. If you have a larger dog, make sure it can comfortably support its weight.
